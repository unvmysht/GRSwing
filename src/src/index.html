Regular Local Events:

Tuesdays

Join the Grand Rapids Original Swing Society for an evening of Social and Swing Dancing. All summer long at Rosa Park Circle in downtown Grand Rapids. When weather does not permit the event is moved indoors to either the Masonic Temple or the GR Public Museum. The event starts with a half hour East Coast Swing lesson at 7 PM and the dancing continues until 10 PM. Cost is $4 per person.

Enjoy another night of live Jazz music at the hands of the John Shea Trio, this time at Big O's Cafe. The band plays from 7-10 PM so come on out and enjoy some music while having a great dinner.

Friday

Enjoy Social Dancing a the Social Dance Studio every Friday night from 8-11 PM. Cost is $7. Music is varies between Waltz, Foxtrot, Tango, Swing, Hustle, Samba, Rumba Cha Cha, Night Club 2-Step, Salsa & Line Dancing.

Join the social dancing at Moveir Dance Studio every Friday night from 7-11 PM. Cost is $10 or $7 with a student ID.

Enjoy live Blues music at Billy's Lounge every Friday night from 9PM - 1 AM. $5 gets you in the door. Must be 21+.

Enjoy live Jazz and Blues music from a house band at the French Quarter. Admission is $10, music starts at 9 PM.

Saturday

Learn some new dance steps at Moveir Dance Studio Saturday night from 7-8 PM followed by open dancing until 11 PM. Cost is $10 for the lesson. Cost for dance is $10 or $7 with a student ID.

Enjoy live Blues music at Billy's Lounge every Friday night from 9PM - 1 AM. $5 gets you in the door. Must be 21+.

Enjoy live Jazz and Blues music from a house band at the French Quarter. Admission is $10, music starts at 9 PM.

Joing DANCEgr at least one Saturday a month (sometimes more) for an evening of social dancing out on the down. Visit DANCEgr.com for more info.

Sunday

Learn some new dance steps at Moveir Dance Studio Sunday evening from 2:30-3:30 PM followed by open dancing until 6 PM. Cost is $10 for the lesson and $10 for the dance.

Dance with local singles at Lincoln Country Club every Sunday night in the lower level ballrooms from 7-10PM. Cost is $7, with a cash bar. Music played is a variety of ballroom styles.
